Setup

LocalLens needs to download AI models to analyze and search your images locally. All processing happens on your machine — nothing is sent to the cloud.

⚠ The download is approximately 2 GB. Make sure you have enough disk space at the chosen location.

Hardware backend used by the AI models. cpu is the default and always-safe choice — it works on any machine. If your hardware supports it, you can try the auto-detected backend (or another compatible option) for potentially much faster results. If it turns out unstable or runs out of memory, simply switch back to cpu.
